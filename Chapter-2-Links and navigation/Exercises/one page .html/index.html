BREAKING BAD

"I am the danger."

Overview

Breaking Bad follows Walter White, a meek high school chemistry teacher who transforms into a ruthless drug kingpin after being diagnosed with terminal lung cancer. To secure his family's financial future, Walter teams up with former student Jesse Pinkman to manufacture and sell crystal meth.

Set in Albuquerque, New Mexico, the series explores Walter's descent into the criminal underworld, showing his gradual transformation from a sympathetic everyman into a feared drug lord known as "Heisenberg." The show's five seasons chronicle Walter's moral decay and the consequences of his actions on everyone around him.

Main Characters

  • Walter White (Heisenberg): A brilliant chemist turned meth manufacturer whose cancer diagnosis leads him to a life of crime.
  • Jesse Pinkman: Walter's former student and partner in the meth business, struggling with addiction and guilt.
  • Skyler White: Walter's wife who becomes increasingly entangled in his criminal activities.
  • Hank Schrader: Walter's DEA agent brother-in-law who leads the investigation into Heisenberg.
  • Saul Goodman: A morally flexible lawyer who becomes Walter and Jesse's "problem solver."
  • Gus Fring: A meticulous and ruthless drug kingpin who becomes Walter's employer and later rival.
  • Mike Ehrmantraut: Gus's fixer and security expert, a former cop with his own moral code.
